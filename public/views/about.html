WORK

The way we work is simple and structured with three clear stages determining positive changes. We always tailor to the case in hand and always aim to maximise good.

Appreciate

Our initial step is to gather information. We need to understand your current strategy and consumption. To do this, you will need to sign a Letter of Authority (LOA) which allows us to check this data. Knowing what there is to know, allows us to understand whats happening now and vitally, why changes will benefit your organisation.

Analyse

From what we know, where do we go? We qualify your consumption. We check you're on the right meter profiles. We review the cost of your existing tariffs and identify potential lower priced tariffs which will save you money. We develop an understanding of your processes and advise on a strategy that aligns with your budgetary needs.

Action

Time to make some changes.

When we've benchmarked your spend and identified reductions in consumption opportunities, we help establish a sustainable strategy that provides you with a clear and logical, stress free operation.

The Bribery Act 2010 - Utility Aid Statement

The Bribery Act 2010 came in to force on 1st July 2011. Utility Aid Limited's (UA) Policy is to conduct all of our business in an honest and ethical manner. We take a zero-tolerance approach to bribery and corruption. UA is committed to acting professionally, fairly and with integrity in all our business dealings and relationships wherever we operate and implementing and enforcing effective systems to counter bribery.

UA condemns all acts of bribery or corruption; any cases brought to its attention will be investigated thoroughly and dealt with appropriately. All employees and all individuals working on behalf of, under contract from or in partnership with UA, or with any of its employees, are expected to be aware of their responsibilities and the standard of behaviour expected.
